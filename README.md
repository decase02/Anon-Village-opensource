# Próspera Security & Hardware Research Enclave 🌴🛡️

Welcome to the public overview repository for the **Security & Hardware Research Enclave**—a proprietary, sub-10 acre physical footprint located within the Próspera jurisdiction on the island of Roatán. 

This project aims to build the ultimate physical hub for the technical community: a high-utility research enclave optimized for deep technical work that often faces regulatory, logistical, or infrastructural friction in standard environments. 

## 🎯 The Vision
We are not building a traditional "gated community." We are building a **high-utility research enclave** and "clean room" for edge-case security research, hardware-in-the-loop testing, and adversarial AI orchestration. It is designed to host top-tier security professionals for 2-to-4 week intensive sprint cohorts and long-term research residencies.

## 🏗️ Core Infrastructure Pillars
This repository outlines the high-level planning for three main developmental pillars:

1. **Heavy Iron & Lab Infrastructure:** 
   * High-performance, redundant micro-grid power (solar + battery storage).
   * Dedicated, climate-controlled server/compute environments.
   * Faraday-shielded spaces and hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) testing labs.
2. **The Regulatory Sandbox (Legal Engineering):** 
   * Leveraging the local Próspera governance framework to protect edge-case vulnerability research and privacy-tech development.
   * Corporate structuring and operational compliance frameworks.
3. **Modular Co-Living & Workspace:** 
   * Scalable, rapid-deployment modular architecture (timber, steel, container).
   * Open-air collaborative "Hacker Plazas" tailored for the Caribbean climate.

**ENCLAVE OPERATING BYLAWS & CORPORATE STRUCTURE**

**Article I: Membership Tiers & Access Controls**
* **Section 1.01 - Digital-to-Physical Parity:** Physical access to the Enclave requires active, unrestricted "Villager" status in the digital terminal. Revocation of digital access automatically revokes physical access rights and triggers immediate eviction protocols.
* **Section 1.02 - The Journalist Exemption:** Credentialed journalists granted access under the "Free Press Exemption" are explicitly barred from entering designated "Clean Room" labs or accessing proprietary vulnerability databases without a multi-signature approval from the Founding Council.

**Article II: Intellectual Property & "Clean Room" Protocol**
* **Section 2.01 - Sovereign IP:** Any code, vulnerability, or hardware spec developed entirely by a Villager remains their sovereign intellectual property. The Village lays no claim to individual research unless developed using Enclave-funded compute or resources.
* **Section 2.02 - The "Spillage" Clause:** In the event that unauthorized malware, black-hat tools, or classified data is introduced into the Enclave’s physical or digital infrastructure ("Spillage"), the responsible Villager assumes 100% financial and legal liability for containment, remediation, and any resulting Próspera Arbitration Center (PAC) arbitration costs.

**Article III: Governance & The Token Treasury**
* **Section 3.01 - The Enclave Treasury:** All governance tokens transferred to the Village pursuant to server rules shall be held in a multi-signature Enclave Treasury. These assets are legally designated for infrastructure development, micro-grid maintenance, and legal defense of the Enclave.
* **Section 3.02 - Voting Rights:** Because token equity is absorbed by the Treasury, governance decisions regarding the physical Enclave shall be conducted on a community-approved or Founding Council basis, entirely decoupled from personal token wealth.

**Article IV: Physical Infrastructure & Liability**
* **Section 4.01 - Infrastructure Indemnity:** Villagers utilizing physical compute clusters, high-voltage equipment, or hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) labs do so at their own risk. The Enclave and its Founders are indemnified against personal injury or hardware bricking resulting from experimental testing.
* **Section 4.02 - Resource Quotas:** Power from the micro-grid and bandwidth from the ISP redundancy network are community assets. The Founding Council reserves the right to throttle or sever compute access to any Villager utilizing disproportionate resources without prior authorization.

**Article V: Dispute Resolution & Expulsion**
* **Section 5.01 - Immediate Suspension:** In the event of an OPSEC breach, physical altercation, or unauthorized commercial shilling, the Founding Council may execute a "Zero-Day Suspension," temporarily freezing digital and physical access pending investigation.
* **Section 5.02 - Binding Arbitration:** Should a suspended Villager contest their expulsion or the forfeiture of their tokens, they must file a formal grievance with the Próspera Arbitration Center (PAC) within 30 days. The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover all arbitration fees.

**Article VI: Corporate Ownership & Legal Representation**
* **Section 6.01 - Entity Sovereignty:** The Próspera Security & Hardware Research Enclave—inclusive of all physical real estate, modular infrastructure, heavy iron compute, and the tokenized Treasury—is the sole and exclusive property of the registered legal entity operating within the Próspera ZEDE jurisdiction.
* **Section 6.02 - The Legal Representative:** Ultimate ownership, fiduciary control, binding signatory power, and executive authority over the legal entity are vested strictly and solely in its officially registered Legal Representative(s) within the Próspera jurisdiction. No decentralized autonomous organization (DAO), voting block of Villagers, or community council can override the fiduciary obligations of the Legal Representative.
* **Section 6.03 - Non-Equity Participation:** Status as a "Villager", physical residency within the Enclave, or the forfeiture of governance tokens to the Village Treasury grants usufructuary (usage) and access rights only. It does not constitute, nor shall it ever be construed as, granting any equity, title, shares, or ownership stake in the legal entity, the physical footprint, or the Enclave’s core infrastructure.
* **Section 6.04 - Dissolution & Liquidation:** In the event of the dissolution of the Enclave or the liquidation of its physical or digital assets, all capital and infrastructure revert entirely to the registered legal entity and its designated Representative. Villagers hold no claim to liquidation dividends.
